I am the author of The Shulamite Bride. A prophet in the area of dreams and dream interpretation. A creative writer and Christian blogger.

God’s love is shared in all our lives.
God’s heart is marriage, family, love. The reason for creation is to share intimacy with God and have fellowship in worship. I share content on various topics.

The Shulamite Bride is a book that was inspired by the Holy Spirit, The Shulamite means Perfect and Peaceable taken from the Song of Solomon TPT translation. The perfect will of God is peace.

The book teaches the heart of God and His love for family. A true family unit is bound by the Love of God. With the correct teaching on marriage and what it entails, kingdom marriage and choosing the right partner according to the blueprint of heaven will enable our future generation not to make the mistakes some of us have made. Jesus is coming back for a church that is without spot or blemish, we have a great responsibility to dress the bride of Christ and get it ready to meet our Bridegroom...Christ, The book is written and designed to get the bride (Church) ready.
